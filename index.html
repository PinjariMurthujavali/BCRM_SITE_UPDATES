BCRM — System Overview

Brilliant CRM on ERPNext — three revenue engines, one unified pipeline

Revenue engine 1 — Product sale

Lead→ Opportunity→ Quotation→ Invoice

Full acquisition pipeline. Always starts with Lead. Opportunity is the main sales tracking unit.

Revenue engine 2 — AMC

AMC created→ Active→ Expiry→ Renewal

Annual Maintenance Contract tied to a product. Created at invoice. Three types: Paid, Complementary, Deferred.

Revenue engine 3 — Renewal / upgrade

Opportunity→ Quotation→ Invoice→ Updated

Product renewals and upgrades happen anytime. Always via Opportunity. No Lead required.

Golden rule

Core principleTrack every opportunity, follow every customer, close every possible revenue.
Pipeline ruleEvery sale = Opportunity. Every call = Activity. Every result = Response. Every closure = Outcome.

Additional Revenue Loops — can happen anytime

These two revenue streams are not tied to the initial sale cycle — they can be triggered at any point during an active customer relationship.

🔁 AMC Sale (any time)
Opportunity→ Invoice→ AMC active

Existing customer who doesn't have AMC can buy it mid-cycle. Create Opportunity (Type: AMC Sale) directly from Customer — no Lead needed.

⬆️ Product Renewal / Upgrade (any time)
Opportunity→ Quotation→ Invoice

Customer wants to upgrade or renew their product license. Create Opportunity (Type: Product Renewal/Upgrade) from Customer Product record — no Lead needed.

Process Flows

Step-by-step breakdown of each major flow

1Lead — enquiry received

Create a Lead record. Capture: Name, Mobile/Email, Source (Incoming Call / Referral / Campaign / Website).

Do NOT create a Quotation at this stage. Lead = qualification only.
DocType: Lead
2Qualification — assess lead

Verify three criteria before proceeding:

Need confirmedBudget availableDecision-maker

If all three pass → convert to Opportunity. Otherwise, mark lead as disqualified.

3Opportunity — main pipeline stage

Convert the qualified Lead to an Opportunity. Fill in: Product (e.g. Tally Prime Gold), Expected deal value, Probability of closing.

Use the full Stage → Activity → Response hierarchy to track every interaction. See the Status Hierarchy tab.

DocType: Opportunity
4Quotation — from Opportunity

Create Quotation directly from the Opportunity record. Include product, pricing, and terms.

Never create a Quotation directly from a Lead. Always from an Opportunity.
DocType: Quotation
5Customer creation

Once the deal is confirmed, convert the Opportunity to a Customer record.

DocType: Customer
6Sales Order + Proforma Invoice

Create Sales Order from the Opportunity. Generate Proforma Invoice for pre-payment confirmation.

DocType: Sales OrderDocType: Sales Invoice (Proforma)
7Payment Receipt

Record payment against the Sales Order. Use Payment Entry to capture payment mode and amount.

DocType: Payment Entry
8Sales Invoice + AMC creation

Generate the final Sales Invoice. Immediately after submission, create the Customer Product record and set up AMC with start date, expiry, and type.

Set AMC type at this stage — Paid, Complementary, or Deferred. This drives all future renewal reminders.

Create a new Opportunity from the Customer record. Set Opportunity Type = Renewal. No Lead creation needed.

Once payment is received and AMC is renewed, the contract re-enters the Active / Service Period. No further sales action until the next expiry reminder cycle fires (30 / 15 / 7 days before the new expiry date).

Payment received→ New AMC active→ Service period (no sales)→ Next expiry reminders

✅ AMC Active — Service Period Rule

Once an AMC is set to Active, it enters a pure service period. No sales activity should be initiated during this phase. Automated reminders fire as expiry approaches. Sales follow-up begins only after a reminder fires with no customer response.

AMC Active→ Service period (no sales push)→ Pre-expiry reminders fire→ Expiry Day→ Post-expiry follow-up→ Renewed ✓

📡 Notification Channels — WhatsApp · SMS · Email

All reminder types are sent across three channels simultaneously. Each channel serves a different purpose in the customer engagement mix.

💬 WhatsApp

Highest open rate. Use for all critical reminders. Rich message with product name, expiry date, and renewal link or CTA button.

📱 SMS

Reliable fallback. Reaches customers without smartphone data. Keep message under 160 chars with product name, expiry, and callback number.

Universal reachNo internet needed
